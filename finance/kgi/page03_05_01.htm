Market Vectors Vietnam ETF is an exchange traded fund incorporated in the USA. The Funds objective is to replicate as closely as possible the price and yield performance of the Market Vectors Vietnam Index. The Fund invests at least 80% of its total assets in securities that comprise the Funds benchmark index.
